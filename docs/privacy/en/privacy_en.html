Privacy Policy

Welcome to the "I'm Alive" application. Respecting your privacy is the core principle of our design. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the app.

1. Data Collection

  • This app does not collect any personal information.
  • App features only record check-in status on the local device (when the user opens the app or switches from background to foreground).

2. Data Usage

  • All local data is used only for:
    • Displaying the current check-in status
    • Triggering automatic check-in and missed check-in notifications
  • It is not used for advertising, marketing, or third-party analysis.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on the user's device.
  • Uninstalling the app or clearing data will result in the deletion of all information.
  • This app does not upload any data to servers.

4. Third-party Sharing

  • This app does not share, sell, or trade any data with third parties.

5. Policy Updates

  • We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the app after updates indicates your agreement to the latest version.
  • Updates will be displayed on the settings page for user review.

Thank you for your trust and use.
